DIRECT INCOME

Referral program has three types of Income. Direct Income is a first one and comes from your own Referrals.

When filling the first and second Slots on any Platform of any Step, You receive Direct Income in 75% of their cost.

LEVELS

The growth of your income exponentially gives a unique system of levels. In total, the Smart Contract of the Referral Program has 16 levels.

Upon registration, Your Platforms are Level 1 and only have two Auto-Updates. After filling the ninth slot, auto-renewal will not occur and you may miss the income from registering new and Auto-Renewing Platforms of existing Referrals.

At any moment You can take the Slot of the next Level. It gives a Platform of three Slots of the new Level, two of its Auto-Updates and an infinite Auto-Update of the Platforms of the previous Levels.

Slots of the new Level cost twice as much as the previous one and bring twice as much Direct Income. Moving up the levels of your Referrals will bring more and more income to You.

What is Crypto?

Cryptocurrency is a digital payment system that does not involve banks in verifying transactions. It is a peer-to-peer system that allows anyone, anywhere, to send and receive payments. When transferring funds in cryptocurrency, transactions are recorded in a public ledger names Blockchain. Cryptocurrency is stored in digital wallets.

What is Smart contract?

Smart contract (intelligent, smart contract) is a computer protocol that allows transactions and controls their execution using mathematical algorithms. Smart contracts are stored on the blockchain platform.

When concluding a smart contract, the parties prescribe the terms of the transaction in it and put their digital signatures. The smart contract independently determines whether the conditions are met and makes a decision in accordance with them.
