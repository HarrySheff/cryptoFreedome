    Blockchain Check

    Please connect to the BSC

    The Cryptolife Smart Contract published in it. You need switch your current Blockchain in the Wallet to the BSC.

    If you don't have this chain in the Wallet you can add it manualy in the Wallet Settings.

    For MetaMask just click on "Networks", choose "Custom RPC" and fill fields by configuration below.

    The BSC configuration:

    • Network Name: Smart Chain
    • New RPC URL: https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org/
    • ChainID: 56
    • Symbol: BNB
    • Block Explorer URL: https://bscscan.com
